BearHub FAQ

How do I earn points for events that I have gone to? ↓

As a student, you can earn points by attending various events.
To do this, simply request points next to the event name on the home page.
The request is then sent to an administrator for approval or denial.

Where do I see how many points I have? ↓

To see the amount of points that you currently have, you must hover over your name that is in the top right of the header tab.
From there, you will be able to see how many points the user currently has along with their grade level.

(ADMIN) How do I give points to students who have attended events? ↓

To give points to students who have attended school events, they must first request the points from their student account.
From there, you are able to approve or deny their request for the points.

How do I know that my points request has been approved or denied?↓

When a Admin accepts or rejects your request, the email that you provided with, will receive an update of the result of the event that you requested;
either Accepted or denied.

How do I redeem prizes using my points? ↓

To see the prizes you are able to redeem with your points, you must go to your home page. On your home page there is a table that displays all of the prizes currently available.
When you click “Claim” on the prize, the points will be taken out of your account, and your request for the prize will be sent to school admin.

How do I see my points report? ↓

To see the points report, you must click on the “Report” button next to your name on the header bar.
The button will take you to a page where you will be able to see a graph showing each grade level and the amount of points they earned, and a ranked listing of the entire school and each grade level and the students who have the top points for each category.
